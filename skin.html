What is Carbon Spectra Peel?

The Carbon Spectra Peel, often called the "Hollywood Peel," is a non-invasive laser skincare treatment that uses a layer of liquid carbon applied to the skin. The carbon absorbs the laser energy, which vaporizes it along with dead skin cells, oil, and impurities, while also stimulating the deeper skin layers. It's quick—often taking 30 minutes—and requires no downtime, making it a popular "lunchtime facial."

What are the Benefits?

  • Clears out gunk for a fresh, bright look right away
  • Makes big pores look smaller and less noticeable.
  • Softens tiny lines and rough patches.
  • Cuts down oil and germs to help with breakouts.
  • Tightens by waking up your skin's bounce.
